Why use congoprize?

1.101% Free

 The bot all features all free nothing is premium or anything

The bot is always been moderated by a human whenever online.

3. Easy to use bot

The bot is easy to use just create a giveaway in this format:

p#start [channel-name] [Time] [winners] [Prize]

Notice: Don't put the brackets
